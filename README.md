ClinovaRx is a modern, responsive personal healthcare platform designed to showcase the professional profile and clinical expertise of a licensed pharmacist. The platform emphasizes patient-centered care, medication safety, and therapeutic excellence while maintaining a clean and trustworthy digital presence.

Built with a focus on both professional branding and practical healthcare value, ClinovaRx highlights real-world clinical experience, academic contributions, and core pharmaceutical competencies.

🚀 Key Features
🏥 Professional pharmacist portfolio and personal branding
💊 Clinical expertise showcase (medication management, patient counseling, therapeutic planning)
📚 Academic experience and teaching contributions
📊 Structured experience timeline (clinical, community, academic)
📞 Contact and consultation-ready interface
🎨 Modern healthcare-inspired UI/UX (clean, responsive, accessible)
🎯 Purpose

The goal of ClinovaRx is to establish a strong digital presence for a clinical pharmacist while enabling future expansion into:

Online patient consultations
Medication awareness content/blogs
Healthcare service integrations
